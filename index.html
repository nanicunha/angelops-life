How AngelOps Works

📤

Step 1 – A Simple Photo

The caregiver sends a photo of your loved one via WhatsApp to a dedicated AngelOps number. No logins, no apps — just one photo starts everything.

🧠

Step 2 – Smart Detection

Our system detects the emotional state, activity, location, and time — using advanced AI powered by Amazon Rekognition. Even multiple photos are automatically analyzed and categorized throughout the day.

📊

Step 3 – Seamless Reporting

All information is sent to your private dashboard — no need to manage or request anything. You receive daily or weekly reports with photos, activities, and emotional insights.

🎁

Step 4 – Monthly or Weekly Gifts

AngelOps sends a personalized gift to your loved one in your name. You can choose monthly or weekly deliveries using a prepaid balance. Each gift is followed by a real-time photo and emotional reaction.

AngelOps connects hearts through intelligent care — no stress, no micromanaging, just peace of mind.

*What are Emotional Surprise Moments?

These are thoughtful, curated experiences designed to brighten your loved one’s day and deepen your connection — no matter the distance. Based on their emotional profile and preferences, we create joyful moments like a playlist from their youth, an afternoon tea with flowers, a surprise art activity, or a heartfelt message from you delivered in a touching way.

Each moment is crafted with love and delivered with the help of our trained caregivers — captured and shared in real-time through AngelOps.
